## Finding RSS Feeds Yourself

1. Try `site.com/rss` or `site.com/feed`
2. Search: `<site name> rss feed url`
3. Look for RSS/feed icon in page source

## Alternative Access Methods (when RSS isn't available)

1. **PressReader** — `nytimes.pressreader.com`, `wsj.pressreader.com` — shows newspaper front pages
2. **Google News** — `news.google.com/search?q=site:nytimes.com+<topic>` — aggregates with excerpts
3. **X/Twitter** — search `site:nytimes.com` for recent article links
4. **Apple News** — RSS format available at various mirror endpoints
